Privacy Policy

Genuine Dining by Genuine Foods

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

Genuine Foods ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Genuine Dining mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. You may optionally provide a student ID number to link your university meal plan.

Profile and Preferences. You may provide dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, halal) and allergen information (e.g., gluten, dairy, nuts). You may also upload a profile photo.

Order and Transaction Data. We collect information about orders you place through the App, including items ordered, station location, order time, and order status.

Engagement Data. We collect data related to your use of App features such as poll votes, staff kudos, food swipe preferences, and theme proposals.

Loyalty Program Data. We track points earned and redeemed, your reward tier, and activity that contributes to your loyalty balance.

Device and Usage Information. We automatically collect device type, operating system, app version, crash reports, and general usage analytics to improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and fulfill food orders
  • Display meal plan balances and dining dollar amounts
  • Personalize menus based on your dietary preferences and allergen settings
  • Operate the loyalty and rewards program
  • Send order confirmations, notifications, and service-related communications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve App features and dining services
  • Diagnose technical issues and monitor App performance

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the following parties:

  • University Partners. We may share limited data with your university's dining or IT department to verify enrollment, link meal plans, and coordinate dining services, in compliance with FERPA.
  • Service Providers. We use third-party services for authentication, email delivery, error monitoring, and analytics. These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

4. FERPA Compliance

For users who are students at partner universities, we treat education-related records in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). We access student information only as authorized by the university and the student, and we maintain appropriate safeguards for education records.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a cloud-hosted database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures including secure authentication, row-level security policies, and encrypted connections. While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update. You can view and update your profile information, dietary preferences, and allergen settings at any time through the App's Profile tab.

Delete Your Account. You can delete your account from the Profile tab. Account deletion permanently removes your personal data, order history, and loyalty points from our systems.

Notifications. You can manage notification preferences through your device settings.

Opt Out of Analytics. You may contact us to opt out of non-essential analytics data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by college and university students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes. We retain transaction records as required by applicable law.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with third-party services including university POS systems for meal plan data. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or sending you a notification. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
